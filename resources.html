Implicit Bias Resource Guide

This guide explores implicit bias, intersectionality, and how unconscious stereotypes influence decision-making in education, healthcare, and employment. Below are key resources to help you understand and challenge implicit bias.

Understanding Implicit Bias

Implicit bias refers to subconscious associations that shape how we perceive others. These biases impact hiring, education, and daily interactions.

Implicit Association Test (IAT)

The IAT measures hidden biases. Take the test to learn about your subconscious associations.
