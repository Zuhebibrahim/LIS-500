Katherine Johnson

Katherine Johnson was a NASA mathematician whose work was critical in the success of U.S. space missions. She played a key role in calculating trajectories for Mercury and Apollo missions.

Key Achievements

  • Helped NASA send astronauts into orbit and land on the Moon.
  • Recipient of the Presidential Medal of Freedom.
  • Portrayed in the film *Hidden Figures*.
